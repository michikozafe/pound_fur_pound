About

Pound FUR Pound is your one stop shop in providing grooming, day care services and pet supplies for your cats and dogs. We are dedicated to quality service, a friendly atmosphere, knowledgeable, caring and experienced groomers, and a clean environment for the safety and comfort of your pet.

We set grooming standards for our groomers as well as pet handling procedures to ensure a safe and pleasant experience. Our attentiveness to the needs of your furry friend means you can guarantee that we are providing an excellent service. We focus on LESS STRESSFUL GROOMING, competitive rates, and customer satisfaction.

Thank you for visiting our website and we are excited to meet you and your pet!
